Hillway Property

What is Commercial Property?

Commercial property refers to buildings and land used for business purposes rather than residential living. This includes offices, retail units, industrial warehouses, shopping centres, and mixed-use developments. Commercial property is an asset class, meaning investors buy and sell it for income (rent) and capital growth, much like shares or bonds. The professionals who advise on this are chartered surveyors, and that is the career path you are now on.

What is RICS?

The Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (RICS) is the global professional body for surveyors. Being RICS-regulated means we adhere to strict ethical standards, professional competence requirements, and client money protection rules. MRICS stands for Member of the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors, which is the professional qualification you will work towards through your APC (Assessment of Professional Competence). RICS regulation affects everything we do: how we handle client money, how we give advice, how we manage conflicts of interest, and how we document our work. Hillway's RICS registration number is 900798.

Hillway Digital

What is PropTech?

PropTech (Property Technology) refers to the use of technology to improve how people buy, sell, manage, and interact with property. This ranges from cloud-based property management platforms to AI-powered analysis tools. The property industry has historically been slow to adopt technology, which creates enormous opportunity for firms like Hillway that can bridge the gap between property expertise and technical capability.

What makes Hillway different from regular tech companies is that we are Chartered Surveyors who build software. We understand the problems because we live them. Every solution we create is evaluated for its potential as a standalone SaaS (Software as a Service) product.

Your Role & Development

Apprentice Surveyor

You will work across both divisions of Hillway, though your primary focus initially will be on the property side. This dual exposure is unusual and valuable: you will understand both the traditional surveying profession and the technology that is transforming it. Very few surveyors your age will have this breadth of experience.

Your Apprenticeship

Starting 30 March 2026, you will begin an MSc Chartered Surveyor apprenticeship at the College of Estates Management (CEM), part of University College of Estate Management (UCEM).

CEM is a distance-learning university that specialises in the built environment. It is the leading provider of surveying qualifications in the UK, and its distance-learning format means you can study while gaining practical experience at Hillway. The course is rigorous and respected across the industry.

Your Path to MRICS

After completing your MSc, you will undertake the APC (Assessment of Professional Competence). This is the final step to becoming a chartered surveyor. The APC involves building a log of professional competencies over a minimum of two years, completing a written submission, and passing a final interview with RICS assessors. Throughout your time at Hillway, you will be building towards these competencies in real client work. When you pass your APC, you earn the MRICS designation — Member of the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors.

What You Will Learn at Hillway

  • Commercial property fundamentals and market dynamics
  • Property management processes and best practice
  • Property inspections and condition reporting
  • Lease advisory: rent reviews, lease renewals, break clauses
  • Service charge management and RICS Code compliance
  • Re-Leased and other PropTech platforms
  • AI tools, automation, and digital transformation
  • How technology transforms traditional surveying practice

Day-to-Day

You will be involved in real client work from the start. Expect to attend site visits, join client calls, prepare reports and analysis, learn to use AI tools for research and writing, and contribute to both property and digital projects. There is no "training bubble" at Hillway; you learn by doing, with guidance and support throughout.
